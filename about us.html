WANT NOW!!!

ANYTHING ANYWHERE IN KIET

WANT NOW is the easy way to get the food you love delivered in the campus of 'KIET' from the Cafeterias in KIET. It is the outcome of the idea given by Ms.Khushi Arora to make KIET fully automatic in terms of food ordering and delivering facilities in KIET. It provides menus and user-reviews/feedbacks of cafeterias in KIET to the users to order their food accordingly by the help of our website. It provides six Cafes to the users to choose their food from:
Green Chilli Cafeteria
Nishu's Cafe
Cafe Coffee Day
Coca Cola
Heroes
Amul Parlour
